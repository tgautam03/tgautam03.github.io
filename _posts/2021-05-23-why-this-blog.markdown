Looking at the title of the blog site, it's easy to comprehend what it'll will be all about. Yes, it'll **mostly** (*not everything though!*) be related to *Machine Learning*. However, instead of tutorials, I'll be taking you through the story of my projects and how I think through different problems. I'll cover *whys* and *hows* of *what* I did in the various projects related to physics, mathematics, computer sciences, etc.  

The main audience for this blog is my future self. I've been working at the intersection of Machine Learning and Geophysics for 3 years now, and when I look back at my old projects (at times the code that I wrote just a few months ago), more often than not, I keep staring at the screen and wonder why I did this or that. So, in future this will act as a *bookmark* and I'll (hopefully!) be able to revisit and think more carefully about the decisions I made to solve any problem set. Apart from this, I also want to share what I do with everyone so that I can get valuable feedback, learn quickly and also help others like me. 

I'll keep this post succinct and hopefully we have an amazing journey ahead of us.
